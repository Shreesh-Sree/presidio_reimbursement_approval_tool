RECEIPT REQUIREMENTS GUIDE
=========================

A valid receipt must include the following information:

REQUIRED INFORMATION:
---------------------
✓ Merchant/Vendor Name
✓ Date of Transaction
✓ Itemized List of Purchases
✓ Total Amount Paid
✓ Payment Method (Cash, Credit Card, etc.)

BEST PRACTICES:
--------------
• Use clear, legible images or scans
• Ensure all text is readable
• Include the entire receipt (don't crop important details)
• For digital receipts, save as PDF when possible
• File size should be under 10 MB
• Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, PDF

EXAMPLES OF VALID RECEIPTS:
---------------------------
- Restaurant bills with itemized food/drinks
- Hotel folios showing room charges
- Taxi/ride-share receipts with route details
- Fuel receipts showing gallons and price per gallon
- Office supply receipts with item descriptions
- Conference/training registration confirmations

INVALID RECEIPTS:
----------------
✗ Credit card slips without itemization
✗ Handwritten notes without merchant info
✗ Blurry or unreadable images
✗ Partial receipts (cut off or cropped)
✗ Receipts older than company policy allows

If your receipt is missing required information, you may need to:
1. Contact the merchant for a duplicate receipt
2. Provide additional documentation (invoice, confirmation email)
3. Submit an expense report explanation form

For questions, contact your expense administrator.
